Questions?

We believe securing your secrets is the first step in securing your whole system.

  • What is Secretr?

    Secretr is a cyber-security start-up which helps protect sensitive keys (i.e. Private Keys, API Keys, etc). We scan your git commits for compromised secrets.

    We report our findings using GitHub Checks to give you feedback directly integrated your code.

    Secretr uses a variety of techniques to detect secrets; which we are continuously improving.

  • Is it safe to use?

    Absolutely! Secretr is premissioned through GitHub. We scan your GitHub source code; however, we never store your code.

    Our sevices run on AWS; with our private keys, and secrets encrypted using KMS.

  • Why should I use Secretr?

    Publishing keys through GitHub is unfortunately too common. This if often the easiest way to breach someone's system.

    Even if your repository is private, it should not be considered safe to store sensitive keys.

    Secretr scans each commit and alerts you if we find potentially leaked secrets.

  • What else should I do to protect myself?

    Secretr will never claim to have a 100% detection rate; as such you need to be responsible for your own secrets.

    We recommend following git best practices in conjunction with Secretr.

  • Where can I provide feedback?

    We'd love to hear from you. Feel free to email us at info@secretr.co at any time.

  • How much does it cost?

    During our beta period; our plans are free! We will always include a free trial on paid tiers, full pricing tiers will be available upon full release.
